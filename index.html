About Company

Horizon Asset Management Company (AMC) was founded in 1995 by a group of experienced investment bankers and financial professionals who had a vision of creating a world-class banking and financial services company in India. The company started with a small team of experts who focused on providing customized financial solutions to clients. With its innovative financial strategies and unique client-centric approach, Horizon AMC quickly gained recognition in the Indian financial industry. The firm's strong reputation in the industry, combined with its focus on delivering high-quality services to clients, helped it to become one of the leading banking firms in India. Over the years, Horizon AMC has continued to expand its service offerings and has now become a one-stop-shop for all financial services, including banking, investment management, and insurance. The company has also expanded its presence globally, with offices in several countries around the world. Today, Horizon AMC is recognized as a top banking firm in India, serving clients from all walks of life. The company's success can be attributed to its innovative financial strategies, unique client-centric approach, and a relentless focus on delivering high-quality services to clients.
